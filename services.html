Frequently Asked HVAC Questions

How often should I service my HVAC system?

We recommend servicing your HVAC system at least twice a year — once in spring before cooling season and once in fall before heating season. Regular maintenance extends the life of your system, improves efficiency, and prevents costly breakdowns.

What is air balancing and why do I need it?

Air balancing is the process of adjusting your HVAC system's airflow to ensure even temperature distribution throughout your home or building. It eliminates hot and cold spots, improves comfort, and can significantly reduce your energy bills by making your system run more efficiently.
